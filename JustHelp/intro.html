Your Journey starts here...

No matter your situation, you can always make it better. But there's no reason to do it alone. With this service, you can find addiction support for you or a loved one. At JustHelp booths, you can find information on needle exchanges, free or reduced price addiction support, rehabilitation clinics, shelters, and vital information from fellow addicts and users.

If you need help immediately and cannot reach a phone press "Emergency" below have emergency services sent.

Dialing 911

Authorities will be contacted to send police and paramedics to this location. When the microphone icon appears, you may speak directly with dispatchers.
